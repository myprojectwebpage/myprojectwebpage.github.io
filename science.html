I researched about a female scientist, Dr. Rosalind Franklin. I researched her life story, background, and contribution to science. She was a female scientist who first studied DNA with an X-ray. She used crystallography to take pictures of DNA back in the 1950s. She died at the age of 38 in 1958 with ovarian cancer.
